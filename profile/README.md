Hegel is a family of property-based testing libraries that attempts to bring the quality of property-based testing found in [Hypothesis](https://github.com/HypothesisWorks/hypothesis) to every language. It is primarily maintained by Hypothesis developers as part of their work at [Antithesis](https://antithesis.com/), hence the name, but is designed first and foremost to integrate with your own testing on your own infrastructure.

Currently the only hegel library is [hegel-rust](https://github.com/hegeldev/hegel-rust), but we have libraries in various states of completion for Go, C++, OCaml, and TypeScript, and expect to be releasing some of them soon.

If you want to know more, [check out the website](https://hegel.dev/).
